# How to write a SaaS headline that doesn't sound like AI

The default AI headline for any SaaS is some arrangement of *powerful, seamless, all-in-one, empower, unlock, streamline.* It's grammatical and completely forgettable. Here's the fix, run through the three questions.

## Before

> **The all-in-one platform that empowers teams to work smarter.**

- **Can I visualize it?** No. "Work smarter" is air — you can't picture it.
- **Can I falsify it?** No. There's nothing here to prove true or false.
- **Can nobody else say it?** No. Every SaaS on earth could sign this.

Three no's. It's slop.

## After

> **Your team's inbox, sorted before they wake up.**

- **Visualize?** Yes — you see the empty inbox at 7am.
- **Falsify?** Yes — it either sorts overnight or it doesn't.
- **Nobody else?** Yes — it's specific to what *this* product does.

## The move

Redpen took the abstract verb ("work smarter") and rewrote it down the page until it hit a concrete outcome ("inbox sorted before they wake up"). Same promise, but now you can see it, prove it, and no competitor can copy it. It also reads in two seconds — one Mississippi, two Mississippi, done.

**Rule of thumb:** if your headline would fit on a competitor's site with zero edits, it isn't a headline yet.
